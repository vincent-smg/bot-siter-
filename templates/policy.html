Privacy Policy

How Anko Uguisu and this website handle your data.

Last updated June 2026

1. What we collect

When you use Anko Uguisu in a Discord server, the bot may store:

  • Your Discord user ID, so we can track levels, XP, and coin balances.
  • Your current Discord username, kept up to date so leaderboards and rank cards display correctly.
  • Server-specific settings configured by moderators, such as welcome channels and word filter lists.
  • Message content only when required for a feature you trigger directly, such as the AI chat commands. Messages are not permanently logged.

2. Signing in with Discord on this website

When you sign in with Discord on this site, we request basic profile information (your user ID, username, and avatar) using Discord's OAuth2 "identify" scope. We do not request access to your email, messages, or servers.

We store your Discord ID and current username so your account stays linked between visits. If we detect that your Discord username has changed, we update our records and may sign you out so the new username takes effect the next time you log in.

3. How we use your data

  • To operate leveling, economy, and moderation features inside Discord.
  • To keep your account and display name accurate on this website.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve the bot's reliability.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties except where required to operate the bot (for example, image hosts used for anime reaction GIFs).

4. Data retention

Leveling and economy data persists so your progress carries between sessions and servers. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us through our support server.

5. Your choices

  • You can remove Anko Uguisu from any server you manage at any time.
  • You can sign out of this website at any time using the "Sign out" button.
  • You can request a copy or deletion of your stored data via the support server.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out on our support server.
